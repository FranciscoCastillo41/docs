## 2026-03-15 — API v1.0

Initial public release.

**Endpoints:**
- Treasury yields — par + TIPS curves, 14 maturities, 1990-present
- Macro observations — GDP, CPI, unemployment, payrolls, fed funds, and more
- Point-in-time queries — `?as_of=DATE` on any macro series
- Revision history — full audit trail for every observation
- Series discovery — search and metadata

**Data coverage:**
- 123,562 treasury yield rows
- 46,461 macro observation rows across 11 series
- Vintage data for all macro series back to first publication
